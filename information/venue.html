Venue Information

The conference will be held at the University of Waikato in Hamilton, New Zealand.

The Waikato Management School building (MSB) will host registration and most activities during the conference.

Parking and Transport options to the university

For information on parking and transport options, see the University's Getting to Campus page. Note carefully that onsite parking has a daily parking fee.

Remember that we will be providing bus transport to and from the university each day if you are staying at one of the conference hotels.

Maps and Wayfinding


A full map of the campus is also available, including parking zones, as well as a map of campus approach routes.
