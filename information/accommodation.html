Accommodation

Conference Hotels

The 12th ACM International Conference on Distributed and Event-Based Systems has arranged a special deal on accommodation with the Novotel Tainui and Ibis hotels.

Located on the banks of Waikato River, both Novotel Tainui and Ibis Hamilton offer warm and generous suites with ensuite bathroom, a flat-screen TV and an iPod docking station. Both hotels are in the heart of the city and offer guests easy access to shopping, dining and entertainment outlets. Guests can relax with a stroll along the river, or enjoy a fun afternoon in one of the many parks surrounding the hotels.

A bus will be available to transport delegates to and back from the University each day.

Novotel Tainui Hamilton

7 Alma Street, Hamilton CBD, Hamilton, New Zealand

Novotel Superior Room (1 King or 2 Double Beds)
A room which mixes modern décor, ergonomic comfort and high end facilities: broadband internet access, iPod docking station, 42 inch flat screen television, mini-bar, tea & coffee making facilities, SKY TV, pay per view movies, air conditioning, iron & ironing board as well as an in-room safe.
