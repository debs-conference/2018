Second International "Events Meet Processes" Workshop

Co-located with DEBS: June 25th, 2018

The "Events Meet Processes" workshop is a cross-disciplinary event to gather the Event Processing (EP) and the Business Process Management (BPM) communities together.

The program will consist of a series of talks that are usually visionary in nature with open-ended observations for the audience. Between each talk, there will be discussion rounds to explore the topic presented. To conclude the workshop, a final discussion session will be held where the audience can propose other topics to be discussed among the participants.

If you would like to give a talk for the workshop, please send a short abstract to the organisers through the dedicated workshop website at: https://epbpm.github.io/index.html.

DEBS2018 25–29 June 2018, Hamilton, New Zealand
