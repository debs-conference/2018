Registration

We must receive at least one non-student conference registration for each paper, accepted in the research, industry, or grand challenge track. For each accepted poster, demo or PhD symposium paper, there must be at least one student or non-student conference registration. If the registration is not received in time, the paper will not be included in the proceedings.
